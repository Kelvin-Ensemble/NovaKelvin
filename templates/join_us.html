Auditions

We’re really excited to see that you’re interested in applying for Kelvin. At Kelvin we wish for the orchestra to be as satisfying and challenging as possible while keeping things fair for all members. To achieve this, the orchestra is auditioned new each year, regardless if you are a new or previous member.

The vast majority if the members are not music students but from other disciplines the likes of the sciences, engineering and the humanities. We also have members from other universities in the Glasgow area like the University of Strathclyde and the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland (RCS)

Every year we run an audition cycle during the first few weeks of the academic year. For more information see below, and keep an eye on our socials!
